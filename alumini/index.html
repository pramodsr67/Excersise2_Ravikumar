Welcome to the VHS Alumini Portal

“Memories linger. Sweet memories linger longer.”

Vikas High School has grown tremendously the last fifty years and so has its student force. Students have disseminated the values of a true Vikasanite as is evident in whatever vocation they have pursued. CEOs, CFOs, actors, businessmen, doctors, software professionals are the diverse alumni we have globally.

To commemorate the 25 years of our progress as an instituition, we invite our former students to reconnect with their Alma Mater.We thought the time is right to build a vibrant network to share mutual progressive views.

Let us relive those nostalgic moments and trace the path of our progress together. And yes, do spread the good word around.

Mr.Krishna Murthy

DIRECTOR
